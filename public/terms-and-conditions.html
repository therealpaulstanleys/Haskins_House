Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: March 14, 2024

1. Purchasing Records

When purchasing items from Haskins House Records:

  • All sales are final on used records, but we will accept returns on sealed new records within 7 days with receipt
  • We guarantee all used records to be playable unless otherwise noted
  • Prices are clearly marked and non-negotiable unless part of a advertised sale
  • We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards
  • Local pickup is available for online orders

2. Selling Records to Us

When selling records to Haskins House Records:

  • We offer fair market value based on condition and demand
  • Records must be clean and playable
  • We reserve the right to refuse any items
  • Payment is immediate upon agreement of sale
  • Sellers must be 18 or older with valid ID
  • We cannot accept stolen merchandise

3. Trading Policy

For record trades:

  • Trade value is typically higher than cash value
  • All trades are final
  • We assess trades based on condition and market value
  • Store credit from trades is valid for one year

4. Record Condition Standards

We use the following grading system:

  • Mint (M): Perfect condition
  • Near Mint (NM): Almost perfect, minimal wear
  • Very Good Plus (VG+): Light wear, minimal surface noise
  • Very Good (VG): Moderate wear, some surface noise
  • Good (G): Significant wear, playable but noisy

5. Store Policies

  • We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone
  • No food or drinks near the records
  • Please handle records with care
  • Children must be supervised
  • Store credit expires after one year

6. Online Orders

  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout
  • We pack records carefully to prevent damage
  • Insurance is available on request
  • Local pickup is free
  • Please allow 1-3 business days for processing

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Changes will be posted here and are effective immediately. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of new terms.
