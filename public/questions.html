Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy used vinyl records?

Yes! We're always interested in expanding our collection. We offer fair prices and even accept trades. Stop by the store or give us a call at (740) 771-0017 to discuss your collection.

What condition should records be in?

Condition is everything in vinyl! We accept records in good to mint condition. Records should be clean, dry, and free from major scratches or warping. Unfortunately, we cannot accept damaged or severely worn records.

What else do you buy besides records?

We purchase a variety of music-related items including:

  • CDs and Tapes
  • Musical Instruments (Guitars, Basses, Keyboards)
  • Audio Equipment
  • Music Memorabilia
  • Video Game Consoles

How can I contact you?

We're here to help! You can:

  • Call us: (740) 771-0017
  • Email: support@haskinshouserecords.com
  • Visit us in store during business hours
  • Use our contact form

Do you offer cleaning services?

Yes! We offer professional record cleaning services to help maintain your vinyl collection. Stop by the store for pricing and details.

Can you help me find specific records?

Absolutely! We're happy to help track down specific albums. We can also notify you when items you're looking for come into stock.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Contact us directly or visit our store.
