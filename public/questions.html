Frequently Asked Questions About Vinyl Records

Do you buy used vinyl records?

Yes. We even trade for it. Stop in, or give us a call. We love new records!

I have wet records!

Condition is everything. We probably don't need your wet records.

Do you guys buy anything but records?

Yes, we do! Besides the obvious tapes and CDs, we often purchase guitars, basses, keyboards, video game consoles, and various memorabilia!

Who do I reach out to if I have questions?

You can contact us by sending an email, or call the store at 740-771-0017.
