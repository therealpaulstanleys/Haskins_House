Our Story

About Haskins House Records

Founded in 2015, Haskins House Records began with a profound legacy - the cherished record collection of the owner's father. What started as a deeply personal tribute has grown into Portsmouth's premier vinyl destination, where each record carries not just music, but memories.

Travis Haskins transformed his father's collection into a community treasure, creating a space where music lovers can discover both rare vinyl and new releases. The store embodies the spirit of preservation, passion, and the timeless connection between generations through music.
