About Haskins House Records: Your Local Vinyl Community

Founded in 2023, Haskins House Records is more than just a record store. We're a thriving community of music enthusiasts dedicated to preserving the art of analog sound and celebrating vinyl culture.

Our Mission: Connecting Music Lovers Through Vinyl

At Haskins House Records, we're on a mission to connect passionate music lovers with high-quality vinyl records and foster a vibrant local music scene. We believe in the power of music to bring people together and create lasting memories.
