About Haskins House Records: Your Local Vinyl Community

Founded in 2015, Haskins House Records is owned and operated by Travis Haskins. The store not only sells, but buys (and trades for) vinyl. If you have a large collection and are wanting to sell it, you can call the store directly at (740)771-0017.

We thrive to bring you the rare, vintage, and even new vinyl! Going through the store, one can find everything from the highest end and rarest discs, to new reprints that won't break the bank. Not onl;y that, but we have tapes, CDs, and 45s. Also, if you are looking for stereo equipment, browse our selection of used decks.
