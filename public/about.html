About Haskins House Records: Your Local Vinyl Community

Founded in 2015, Haskins House Records is owned and operated by Travis Haskins. The store not only sells, but buys (and trades for) vinyl. If you have a large collection and are wanting to sell it, you can call the store directly at (740)771-0017.
