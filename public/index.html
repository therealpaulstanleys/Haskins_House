About Haskins House Records

Haskins House Records is your go-to destination for vinyl records and a vibrant music community. Established in 2015, we've been dedicated to providing music enthusiasts with a curated selection of vinyl records and fostering a shared love for music.

Visit our store to explore our extensive collection of vinyl records spanning various genres and eras. From classic albums to the latest releases, we have something for every music lover.
