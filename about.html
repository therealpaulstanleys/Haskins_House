About Haskins House Records

Founded in 2015, Haskins House Records has been serving our community's music needs for nearly a decade. Our journey began with a heartfelt legacy - our owner's sole inheritance, his father's cherished record collection.

This personal connection to music has been the driving force behind our passion to share the joy of audio with others. For 10 years, we've been more than just a store; we've been a hub for music lovers and audiophiles alike.

What We Offer

  • New and used vinyl records
  • Cassette tapes
  • CDs
  • Vintage stereo equipment
  • Music memorabilia
  • And much more!

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning your musical journey, Haskins House Records is your go-to destination. We pride ourselves on our curated selection, knowledgeable staff, and our commitment to preserving the rich history of recorded music.

Come visit us and experience the warmth of vinyl, the nostalgia of tapes, and the clarity of CDs. At Haskins House Records, we're not just selling music - we're sharing a legacy, one record at a time.
